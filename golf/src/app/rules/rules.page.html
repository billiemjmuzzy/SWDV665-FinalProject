U.S.G.A. rules shall govern play

Except where modified by local rules

Winter rules apply Out of Bounds: White stakes indicate out of bounds. A penalty is the loss of distance and one stroke. Lost Ball: If a ball is lost the player shall play their next stroke as near as possible to the spot from which the original ball was lost or moved by another player, adding a penalty stroke to their score. Water Hazard: Holes 2 and 7, off of the tee. Penalty, stroke, and distance. Lateral Water Hazard (Red staked areas): Holes 1, 4, 5, 6, and 9. If a ball lies or is lost in a lateral water hazard, the player may, under penalty of one stroke, drop ball in line of flight, not near the hole. Unplayable Lie: Under penalty of one stroke, you may lift the ball and drop within two club-lengths where the ball lies, but not near the hole. Grass area around water hazards denotes water hazard limits. A ball lying in newly seeded areas near new trees or shrubs, in casual water, or near obstructions may be removed to playable turf not nearer to the hole in the line of flight without penalty. Please drive and park at least 15 ft. from greens.
